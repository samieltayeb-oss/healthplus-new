Overview & Care Focus

You will connect with a physician via a secure video or phone link. They will assess your symptoms, provide advice, or determine if an in-person visit is required.

Who This Is For

Existing patients with appropriate, non-emergency concerns that do not require a physical examination.

Preparation & What To Bring

Ensure you have a stable internet connection, a quiet and private space, and your health card ready.
