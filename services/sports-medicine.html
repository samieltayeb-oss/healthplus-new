Injury & Recovery

Sports Medicine

Diagnosis, treatment, and recovery planning for activity-related injuries to help you return to motion safely.

Overview & Care Focus

A physical assessment of your injury, mobility, and strength. Your doctor will discuss imaging if necessary and create a structured recovery timeline.

Who This Is For

Athletes, active individuals, or anyone experiencing pain or injury related to physical activity or exercise.

Preparation & What To Bring

Wear comfortable, loose clothing that allows easy access to the injured area. Bring any previous imaging or notes related to the injury.

Frequently Asked Questions

Not at all. We treat anyone with an activity-related injury, from weekend warriors to daily walkers.
Our physicians diagnose and plan your recovery. We coordinate closely with trusted physiotherapists in the community for your rehabilitation.

Conditions & Focus

  • Sprains, strains, and joint pain
  • Overuse injuries like tendonitis
  • Minor fractures and dislocations
  • Return-to-sport planning
