Specialist

Dr. Angela Vinturache

Dr. Angela Vinturache is an obstetrician-gynecologist whose practice blends attentive, relationship-centred care with the best available evidence.  She provides comprehensive care from adolescence through the post-menopausal years, beginning with routine gynecologic care and the assessment of common concerns such as abnormal uterine bleeding and pelvic discomfort. Her clinical work also includes menopause care (symptom relief, cardiovascular and bone health, and individualized therapy), contraception and family planning—including counselling for routine and complex needs—infertility assessment and management, pre-conception counselling, prenatal and postpartum care, and expertise in high-risk obstetrics and maternal medicine.  Consultations focus on understanding each person’s priorities, explaining options in plain language, and building care plans together. Patients describe her style as warm, thorough, and collaborative; whether discussing contraception, navigating menopausal symptoms, planning a pregnancy, or reviewing test results, Dr. Vinturache aims to ensure every patient leaves with a clear plan and confidence in their next steps.  She is equally committed to patient education, safety culture, and quality improvement in women’s health. A clinician–scientist, she brings a wealth of experience from Oxford, London, and Dublin, and is actively involved with provincial and national professional bodies to develop guidelines, promote and advocate for women’s health, and continually improve the quality of care.

Specialist

Dr. Bhavisha Ramsamy

• I am a South African–qualified pediatrician with over ten years of experience dedicated to pediatrics and child health. I hold a Master of Medicine in Pediatrics from the University of the Witwatersrand and a Fellowship from the College of Paediatricians of South Africa. In addition, I have obtained a Diploma in Child Health and a Diploma in HIV Management through the Colleges of Medicine of South Africa, further strengthening my expertise in comprehensive pediatric care. • My special interest lies in neonatology and the care of the newborn. I completed my specialist training at Chris Hani Baragwanath Hospital, one of the largest hospitals in the world, where I had the privilege of serving vulnerable rural and semi-urban communities in Soweto, South Africa. This experience shaped my clinical approach, grounding it in both evidence-based medicine and compassionate, community-centered care. • Alongside my clinical responsibilities, I have been actively involved in undergraduate and postgraduate medical education at the University of the Witwatersrand, contributing to the training of medical students and pediatric registrars. Teaching has been a deeply rewarding aspect of my career, allowing me to support the development of future healthcare professionals. • As a new mother, I have gained a profound appreciation for the lived experience of parenthood. This personal journey has enriched my understanding of holistic child care, strengthening my empathy and reinforcing my commitment to partnering with families in nurturing healthy, thriving children. • I look forward to embarking on this new chapter at Health Plus Medical, where I am committed to delivering compassionate, high-quality pediatric care and supporting families at every stage of their child’s development.

Specialist

Dr. Tariq Khan

• Dr Tariq Khan is a Consultant Psychiatrist with extensive experience in adult and old age psychiatry across the UK and Canada. He completed his medical foundation and specialty training in the UK and developed expertise in neuropsychiatry, dementia, general adult and adult ADHD. • Before moving to Canada from the UK, he worked as a consultant psychiatrist across NHS, independent sector and Her Majesty’s Prison Services, and served as Medical Lead for inpatient psychiatric services and postgraduate education tutor for psychiatry residents, receiving a LCEA – Clinical Excellence Award. • In Canada, he was faculty and Staff Psychiatrist at the Centre for Addiction and Mental Health Toronto (CAMH) within the Concurrent Disorders Programme, and Assistant Professor at the University of Toronto, where he established a pioneering service for concurrent ADHD in adults. • Dr Khan is an Expert Reference Group member for the Royal College of Psychiatrists’ UK International Diploma, and his work has been published in the College’s faculty newsletters. He now brings his expertise to Health Plus Medical in Calgary, where he provides compassionate, evidence-based psychiatric care.

I am particularly drawn to the diagnostic challenges within infectious diseases, where attention to detail and clinical curiosity are essential in delivering accurate, timely, and compassionate care.• Beyond clinical practice, I am a committed academic and researcher, having presented my work on international platforms.
