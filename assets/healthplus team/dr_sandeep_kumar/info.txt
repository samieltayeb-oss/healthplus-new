Dr. Sandeep Kumar: Your Trusted Family Physician in Calgary About Dr. KumarDr. Sandeep Kumar is a highly experienced and compassionate Family Physician with over 15 years of clinical expertise spanning India, the Republic of Ireland, the UK, and now Calgary. Trained as a family physician in the UK, he was awarded a Certificate of Completion of Training (CCT) and MRCGP and worked there as a full-time General Practitioner. He holds Postgraduate Diploma in Clinical Dermatology from the University of South Wales (USW), UK.Dr. Kumar is passionate about providing comprehensive care for individuals and families of all ages. Whether it’s managing chronic conditions, addressing acute health concerns, or promoting preventive care, Dr. Kumar ensures his patients receive personalized, evidence-based treatment in a warm and welcoming environment. What Dr. Kumar Offers: Primary Care for All Ages: From newborns to seniors, Dr. Kumar provides holistic care for the whole family.Chronic Disease Management: Expertise in managing conditions such as diabetes, high blood pressure, and asthma.Preventive Care: Helping patients stay healthy through screenings, vaccinations, and health education.Women’s Health: Support for family planning, prenatal care, and other women’s health needs. Dr. Kumar is fluent in English, Hindi, Punjabi, Urdu, and basic Armenian, which allows him to connect with patients from diverse backgrounds and provide culturally sensitive care. A Personal Note from Dr. Kumar“When I’m not working, I enjoy spending time outdoors with my family, listening to music, and playing table tennis and pool. My goal as a physician is to provide not just excellent medical care but also a positive and reassuring experience for every patient. I look forward to being a part of your health journey and supporting your well-being.” Dr. Kumar is currently accepting new patients.Contact us today to book an appointment and take the first step toward your best health!
