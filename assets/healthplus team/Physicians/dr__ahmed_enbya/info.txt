Information:
Dr. Ahmed Enbya is a family physician with over 10 years of experience providing comprehensive, patient-centered care. He recently relocated to Alberta from British Columbia, bringing with him a strong commitment to family medicine. Dr. Enbya has a strong interest in longitudinal care, supporting patients and families through all stages of life and all aspects of health. His dedication to his patients was recognized with a nomination for the BCCFP My Family Doctor 2025 award. Outside of medicine, Dr. Enbya is a proud father of three energetic boys. He enjoys spending time outdoors—whether camping, biking, or capturing moments through photography
