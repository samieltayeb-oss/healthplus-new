Information:
• Dr Kheir is a family physician with interest in emergency medicine, acute & urgent care, chronic disease management, women’s health, sexual & reproductive healthcare minor surgical procedures, as well as bedside ultrasound. 
• He was trained in the UK and obtained his board certification in Family Medicine (MRCGP), as well the Diploma of the Royal College of Obstetrician & Gynaecologists (DRCOG) and the Diploma Faculty of Sexual and Reproductive Healthcare (DFSRH). 
 • Before moving to Calgary, he worked at Johns Hopkins Aramco Healthcare for 5 years as a Family Medicine Consultant and he was the Chief (A) of the Primary Care Department. He was also a COVID team physician lead during the Pandemic.  He was also a faculty board member of the Family Medicine residency program.
