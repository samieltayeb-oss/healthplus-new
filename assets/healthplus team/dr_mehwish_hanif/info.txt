Information:
• Dr. Mehwish Hanif is a Family Physician specializing in Aesthetic Medicine with a focus on preventative care, women’s health, skin wellness, and mental well-being. She graduated from the University of Calgary Medical School in 2016, completing her Family Medicine residency there as well. Certified by the College of Family Physicians of Canada, Dr. Hanif is also a recipient of the CFPC Medical Student Leadership Award and ACFP Outstanding New Professional Award.• In her aesthetic practice, she offers treatments like dermal fillers, PRP therapy for hair and skin rejuvenation, and Botox, addressing both cosmetic and medical concerns such as migraines, TMJ disorders, and hyperhidrosis. She is also an expert in women’s health, providing services including contraception counseling, IUD procedures, prenatal/postpartum care, and gynecological screenings. • With a holistic approach, Dr. Hanif helps patients navigate both physical and psychological challenges. Outside of her medical practice, she enjoys traveling and staying active.
