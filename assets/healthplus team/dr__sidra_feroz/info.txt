Information:
Dr. Feroz completed her medical degree in 2017 from Pakistan, followed by a two-year residency in Family Medicine. She has a special interest in women’s health and community-based care, with a strong focus on providing compassionate, patient-centered medicine for individuals and families across all stages of life.     Originally from Pakistan, Dr. Feroz made Calgary her home two years ago. Outside of the clinic, she enjoys traveling and spending time with her family.
